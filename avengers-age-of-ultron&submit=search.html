)"> Winston W. reviewed Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

Review from the comment

Well, I suppose it couldn't last forever. Avengers: Age of Ultron is the first misstep for me in today's Marvel cinematic universe. I could go over a few things in detail, but I'll keep it short. The script is choppy. What is the plot and story line besides Ultron wants to take over the world? Additionally, do you have to fill every single Marvel film with 20+ jokes? 18 of them aren't even that funny. It's just starting to run a little stale for me. Ultron, awesome villain in the comics, but here it's almost like he shows up out of the blue. I hope that the next time Marvel puts something together, it will be full of substance and not so much flash.
